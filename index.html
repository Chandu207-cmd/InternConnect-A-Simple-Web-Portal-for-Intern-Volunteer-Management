Welcome to the Intern/Volunteer Portal

Welcome to the Intern/Volunteer Portal for the Basti Ki Pathshala Foundation. This platform is designed for individuals passionate about contributing their skills to support our mission of education and community upliftment.

As part of our Full Stack Development Internship, you will gain hands-on experience building, improving, and maintaining digital solutions that help us reach more learners and supporters.

Use this portal to:

  • Apply as an intern or volunteer for the current program
  • Submit required documents and information
  • Stay updated on project tasks and announcements
Admins can access applications, manage submissions, and monitor progress.
